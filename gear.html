Below is a list of food and camp kitchen items I will be bringing with me:

  1. Pocket Rocket 2 Stove and Gas
  2. 1 Ultralight Cooking Pot
  3. Metal Spork
  4. Cambelbak 1.5 OR 3 liter water bladder
  5. Lifestraw

I will be packing 3 liters of clean water in my Camelbak Hydration Pack for the week - when I run out of this, I will be using a LifeStraw water purification system to drink from renewable sources such as brooks and mountain streams.
